Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our AI Video & Photo Editing program.

Do I need prior video editing experience for this course? +
No prior experience is required. We start from absolute basics of video editing and gradually progress to advanced AI tools. The course is designed for complete beginners as well as experienced editors wanting to learn AI tools.
What AI tools will I learn during the course? +
You'll master 8 powerful AI tools: Descript AI (video editing by text), Pictory AI (text to video), Veed AI (auto-subtitles & translations), ChatGPT (script writing), NotebookLM (research), Google Gemini (content creation), Vo3 AI (voice synthesis), and Freepik AI (image generation).
Will I learn how to earn money with these skills? +
Yes! We cover freelance training including how to find clients on Upwork/Fiverr, price your services, create portfolios, handle client communication, and build a sustainable freelance business. Many students start earning within 2 months.
Is there any placement assistance? +
Yes! We provide complete placement assistance including portfolio building, LinkedIn optimization, client referrals, mock interviews, and direct job opportunities with our partner companies in media, marketing, and content creation.
What is the duration and schedule of the program? +
The program is 3 months long with 2-hour classes from Monday to Friday. We also offer weekend batches. Recordings are available for missed classes. Daily practice assignments and weekly real-world projects ensure you build a strong portfolio.
